About DONOR-HUB

We are a non-profit organization that was founded mainly as a donation foundation to give children with unfortunate backgrounds free gear specified to play basketball.

We will be receiving basketball shoes, sport clothes, headbands, wristbands, and other gear related to playing baksetball as donations.

Our Values

MISSION: Our mission is to give every kid the opportunity to reach a higher level in their passions. We focus on giving children sufficient gear in order to play their favorite sport: basketball.

VISION: Our vision is that every child will be able to play basketball without any restrictions.

What We Offer

DONEES: Donees simply have sign up to DONOR-HUB to receive free basketball gear such as shoes and clothes. We offer goods based on supplies, but we generally give once a month.

DONARS: Donars can donate their new or used gear in exchange for D-Tokens. With enough D-Tokens, they can be used to purchase new gear from our sponsor company.
